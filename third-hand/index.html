Third-Hand Play

Third hand high — the mirror of second hand low. Partner has led and plays first; your job is to win the trick or force out declarer's honor, then help partner read the suit.

your play
The four rules
  1. Third hand high. Play your highest to win, or to force out declarer's honor.
  2. Lowest of touching honors. From a sequence, play the cheapest — it shows the ones above it.
  3. Finesse against dummy, never partner. If dummy shows an honor and plays low, insert an intermediate.
  4. Return partner's suit right. Higher of a doubleton, else a low card that shows your length.
